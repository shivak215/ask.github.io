Vacation Package

EXOTIC ANDAMAN

price:₹ 16000

Buy Ticket
(4 days/ 3 nights, departing on every Wednesday, Saturday)

Day 1: Arrive Port Blair
On arrival to Port Blair Airport, you will be greeted by our representative and then transfer to the hotel. After check-in to the hotel take rest for few hours. Evening take a trip to Cellular Jail to experience the spectacular light and sound show. Overnight at Port Blair.

Day 2: Port Blair - Havelock
Leave early morning to the Havelock Island. On arrival to Havelock you will be escorted to the Resort. Afternoon is at leisure. Later visit the Radha Nagar beach. Overnight at Havelock.

Day 3: Havelock
After breakfast visit to Elephant beach by motor boat for mesmerizing view of corals through glass bottom boat and enjoy snorkeling (optional). Overnight stay at resort in Havelock.

Day 4: Havelock - Port Blair- Coral Island - Ross Island - Port Blair
Embark on a Two Island Cruise, visit to the Ross Island North Bay Coral Island. Named after marine Surveyor Sir Daniel Ross it was the Headquarters for the Panel settlement for nearly 80 Years. A well planned township away from the prisoners. After a 2 Hrs trip to Ross Island proceed towards the North Bay Coral island , this is nearest point around Port Blair you can witness some underwater coral , either through Glass Bottom Boats are by Snorkeling. Return to the Port Blair for the overnight stay.
